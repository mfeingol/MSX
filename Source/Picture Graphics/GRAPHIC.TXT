Name: Picture Graphics
Author: Max Attar Feingold (Illuminatus Software)
Year: 1992
Language: Spanish
Platform: MSX2
Category: Utilities / Graphics

Controls:

Cursors, spacebar

Description:

Picture Graphics is a collection of twelve original images ('art') created using Philips Ease during a moment of weakness in which Videographics' image multiplier seemed cool.  The 'artist' obviously never expected the world wide web to make similar images popular in years to come.
